Top Dog Care Tips

1. Balanced Nutrition

Provide high-quality dog food with the right balance of protein, fats, and carbs. Incorporate fresh veggies as treats for a healthy diet.

2. Daily Exercise

Keep your dog active with indoor play: fetch, tug-of-war, or puzzle toys to burn energy and stimulate their mind throughout the day.

3. Consistent Training

Use positive reinforcement to teach basic commands like sit, stay, and come. Consistency builds trust and promotes good behavior.

4. Regular Grooming

Brush fur weekly, trim nails, and clean ears to prevent matting and infections. A clean dog is a happy and healthy dog!
